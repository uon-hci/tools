    Lecture (09/10/2018)

    Variance (S²)

    $$ S^2 = \frac{\sum (x - \bar{x})^2}{N - 1} $$

    Standard Deviation (SD)

    Perfect description of an entire distribution

    $$ S = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x - \bar{x})^2}{N - 1}} $$

    Z scores

    Used to perform calculations on normally distributed data A Z score is the distance from the mean measured in units of Standard Deviation

    $$ Z = \frac{x - \bar{x}}{SD} $$
